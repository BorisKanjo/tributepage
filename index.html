Nelson Mandela

The man who changed South Africa and touched the world

Here is a timeline of Madiba's life:

  • 1918 - Born Nelson Rolihlahla "Madiba" Mandela in Mvezo in Umtata, Cape Province, South Africa
  • 1933 - began secondary education at Clarkebury Methodist High School in Engcobo
  • 1937 - moved to Healdtown to attend the Methodist College at Fort Beaufort
  • 1939 - began working on a Bacholor of Arts degree at the University of Fort Hare, an elite black institution in Alice, Eastern Cape but did not finish
  • 1947 - was elected to the executive committee of the ANC's Transvaal Province Branch
  • 1949 - denied law degree at University of the Witwatersand after failing final year three times
  • 1950 - Became ANC national executive and later elected president of the ANCYL
  • 1962 - arrested with fellow activist and jailed in Johannesburg's Marshall Square prison
  • 1964 - sentenced to life imprisonment and sent to prison on Robben Island remaining there for 18 years
  • 1990 - Mandela leaves Victor Verster prison on February 11th
  • 1994 - was inaugurated president of South Africa where he served till 1999
  • 2013 - died on Decenber 5th at age 95 at his home in Houghton
